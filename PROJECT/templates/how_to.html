How to Apply

Applying for a room in our student dormitory is a straightforward process designed to help you find the perfect living space. Follow these steps to ensure a smooth application experience.

Application Process

Register Your Account

Select and Request a Room

Approval and Profile Check

To begin, create an account on our platform. Provide accurate personal information and verify your student status. Once registered, you can explore available rooms and submit your application. Our administrators will review your request and notify you of the approval status. Finally, ensure your profile is complete and up-to-date for a seamless experience.
